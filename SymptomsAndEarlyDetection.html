Symptoms & Early Detection

Recognize warning signs and take timely action

Malaria
Common Symptoms:
  • High fever (up to 104°F/40°C)
  • Chills and sweating
  • Headache and muscle pain
  • Fatigue and weakness
  • Nausea and vomiting
Early Detection Tip:

Suspect malaria if fever occurs 7-30 days after visiting endemic areas. Rapid diagnostic tests can confirm within 15 minutes.

Dengue Fever
Common Symptoms:
  • Sudden high fever
  • Severe headache
  • Pain behind eyes
  • Muscle and joint pain Characteristic
  • Skin rash
Early Detection Tip:

Watch for "dengue triad": fever, rash, and headache. Low platelet count on blood tests is a warning sign.

COVID-19
Common Symptoms:
  • Fever or chills
  • Persistent cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss of taste/smell Distinctive
  • Fatigue
Early Detection Tip:

RT-PCR tests can detect infection before symptoms appear. Rapid antigen tests are effective 5-7 days post-exposure.

Chikungunya
Common Symptoms:
  • High fever
  • Severe joint pain Debilitating
  • Muscle pain
  • Headache
  • Rash
Early Detection Tip:

Differentiate from dengue by the intensity of joint pain. Symptoms appear 3-7 days after mosquito bite.

Tuberculosis
Common Symptoms:
  • Persistent cough (3+ weeks)
  • Chest pain
  • Coughing up blood
  • Fatigue
  • Weight loss
Early Detection Tip:

Sputum tests and chest X-rays can detect TB. Latent TB shows no symptoms but can be detected via skin or blood tests.

Influenza
Common Symptoms:
  • Sudden fever
  • Dry cough
  • Sore throat
  • Runny/stuffy nose
  • Body aches
Early Detection Tip:

Rapid influenza tests can confirm within 15 minutes. Antiviral treatment works best when started within 48 hours.

General Early Detection Guidelines
When to Seek Medical Help:
  • Fever lasting more than 3 days
  • Difficulty breathing or chest pain
  • Persistent vomiting
  • Confusion or dizziness
  • Severe dehydration symptoms
Prevention Tips:
  • Get recommended vaccinations
  • Practice good hand hygiene
  • Use insect repellent in endemic areas
  • Maintain proper sanitation
  • Report symptoms early to health authorities
